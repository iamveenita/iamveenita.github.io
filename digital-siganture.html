FAQ

What is the requirement of Digital Signature?

The requirement of Digital Signature has its own importance. Digital Signature is used for E-tendering .if anyone is applying for any kind of tender ,then he can used digital signature for filling the tenders. DSC are safe and secure way.

Which Authority Issued The Digital Signature Certificate?

A licensed Certifying Authority (CA) issues the digital signature. Certifying Authority (CA) means a person who has been granted a license to issue a digital signature certificate under Section 24 of the Indian IT-Act 2000.
